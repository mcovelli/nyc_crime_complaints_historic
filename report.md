### Key Insights  

1. Crime declined in 2020, likely due to COVID-19.
2. Brooklyn is consistently the borough with the highest crime rate.
3. Staten Island has consistently significantly less crime than any other borough.
4. Staten Island, Brooklyn have approximately the same crime rate as they did in 2006.
5. Queens, Manhattan and the Bronx have rates higher than their 2006 levels.
6. Overall, crime is up since 2006.
7. From 2007 to 2020 crime had been trending downward
8. Since 2021 crime has been trending upward.
